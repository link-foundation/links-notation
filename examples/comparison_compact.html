Format Comparison

LiNo, YAML

JSON, XML

LiNo
family: (parents: (father: John) (mother: Mary))
family: (children: (son: Tom) (daughter: Alice))
(John loves Mary)
(Mary loves John)
(parents love children)
pets: (dog: Rex) (cat: Whiskers)
(Rex belongs_to family)
(Whiskers belongs_to family)
address: (street: "123 Main St")
         (city: Springfield)
         (state: Illinois)
YAML
family:
  parents:
    father: John
    mother: Mary
  children:
    son: Tom
    daughter: Alice

relationships:
  - subject: John
    predicate: loves
    object: Mary
  - subject: Mary
    predicate: loves
    object: John
  - subject: parents
    predicate: love
    object: children

pets:
  dog: Rex
  cat: Whiskers

address:
  street: "123 Main St"
  city: Springfield
  state: Illinois

LiNo: Natural • Concise • Intuitive • Flexible

Most compact and readable format for representing relationships
